Animal Trip Quiz

In a small village, there are three people: a baker, a butcher and a candlestick maker.

Each person has a pet: the baker has a cat, the butcher has a dog and the candlestick maker has a bird.

One day the Easter Bunny was hopping through town and they all decided to go on a trip together, each bringing along their pet.

Along the way, they passed through four towns.

In the first town, they met a man with two cats.

In the second town, they met a woman with three dogs.

In the third town, they met a child with four birds.

And in the fourth town, they met a farmer with a cat, a dog, and a bird.
