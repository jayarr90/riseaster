Xmas Riddle

I'm draped in strands so fine,
A sparkling trail that seems to shine.
I deck the halls in festive sheen,
Reflecting light with a glimmering gleam.

I twist and turn, a metallic stream,
On branches of green, a festive theme.
I add a touch of magic and grace,
A shimmering dance in a festive space.

What am I?
